Our Story

Born from
Salt & Sun

SABLE began on a sun-bleached afternoon in Bodrum, when our founder realised that the world's finest cotton — grown minutes from the Aegean — never made it into the towels people actually loved.

We partnered directly with third-generation weavers to create something that feels like a warm breeze and lasts a lifetime. No shortcuts, no synthetic blends — just cotton, craft, and coastline.
